Welcome to my homepage!

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, under the supervision of Dr. Yangyang Xu.
Prior to this, I recieved my Ph.D. in Mathematical Sciences from Clemson University in 2018, and my advisor was Dr. Warren Adams. Before then, I recieved my M.S. in Applied Mathematics from University of Nevada, Reno, and B.S. in Mathematics from Nankai University.

My research interests are in all fields of optimization, with particular emphases in continous optimization, mixed-discrete and continuous nonconvex programming.
